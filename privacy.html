Privacy Overview

We collect only the information needed to operate your revenue pod: contact details you share, performance metrics you want tracked, and platform activity that supports the engagement. Everything is stored in encrypted systems with access limited to your Revno operators.

What we log

Account contacts, pipeline metrics, platform usage signals, and support conversations relevant to the engagement.

Why we log it

To run outreach, deliver analytics, personalize coaching, and keep your sales systems synchronized.

Who sees it

Only the Revno team assigned to your account plus vetted automation vendors required to execute the agreed scope.

Data Use & Retention

We use your data solely to deliver the services described in your Statement of Work. We never sell data and do not use it for unrelated marketing.

  • Processors. We rely on secure tools (CRM, automation, communication) under strict access controls.
  • Retention. Data is retained while the engagement is active and removed or returned within 30 days of termination unless law requires more.
  • Security. Credentials are stored in centrally managed vaults, MFA is enforced for operators, and systems are reviewed quarterly.

If you need a Data Processing Agreement or custom retention schedule, request it through your account lead.

Your Rights

You control your information. Share requests with your Revno lead and we’ll coordinate the steps below—responses arrive within five business days.

  • Access or export the data we hold about your organization.
  • Request corrections or restrictions on specific datasets.
  • Delete data at the end of an engagement (subject to legal retention duties).
